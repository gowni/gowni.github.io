Plot Synopsis

Oldboy is a South Korean revenge thriller directed by the greatly talented Park Chan-Wook. This movie revolves around the story of a man who is kidnapped and kept prisoner in isolation for 15 years. After being suddenly and mysteriously released the man tries to find out who kidnapped him and why. His journey on this path forms the rest of the movie

My review

Oldboy is often referred to as one of the best movies of all time, and for good reason. Everything about this movie is almost pure perfection. The story and screenplay are absolutely gripping and will have you hooked right from the start. The action in this movie is great, with one particular fight in a hallway standing out as being iconic. The performances of the actors are all top-notch and will leave you feeling every bit of the emotion that the characters are experiencing. The climax of this movie is one of the best endings I have ever seen in a movie, making the whole movie stick with you long after you watch it. This is a must watch for every film lover out there.
