Plot Synopsis

The Wailing is a horror mystery which tells the story of a police officer in a South Korean village, Jong-Goo. When a Japanese man newly moves into his village, a mysterious infection starts spreading causing people to kill their own families. How Jong-Goo, tackles this infection and what the Japanese man has to do with it forms the rest of the story.

My review

The Wailing is my current favorite horror movie of all time. Rather than throwing some cheap jump-scares at the audience and calling it a day, the film takes its time and contantly builds the tension to provide a great pay-off at the climax. It is definitely a slow-burn which might not appeal to everyone, but it is one of the most well crafted horror stories I have ever seen. I highly recommend everyone to check it out
